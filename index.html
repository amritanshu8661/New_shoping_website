Meet Dr. Corrie Amos

Board-Certified Anesthesiologist & Culinary Enthusiast

Physician-Crafted Excellence

Dr. Corrie Amos, a Jamaican-American anesthesiologist, founded The Spice Theory to combine her medical expertise with her Caribbean heritage. Each spice blend is carefully formulated to deliver maximum flavor while promoting heart health through significantly reduced sodium content.

Our mission is simple: bring you the authentic taste of the Caribbean without compromising your health. Every blend is vegan-friendly, gluten-free, and contains no artificial additives, preservatives, or MSG.

Health Benefits

  • Supports heart-healthy cooking
  • Maintains authentic Caribbean flavors
  • No artificial preservatives or MSG
  • Physician-formulated for optimal nutrition
